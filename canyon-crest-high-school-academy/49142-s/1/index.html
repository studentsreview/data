Canyon Crest High School Academy is located in San Diego, CA. Students and parents can contact Canyon Crest High School Academy by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (858) 481-8221. Canyon Crest High School Academy has 1757 total students, 899 male, 858 female. Of these students from Canyon Crest High School Academy, 424 are ninth grade students, 476 are tenth grade students, 450 are eleventh grade students, and 407 are twelfth grade students. Canyon Crest High School Academy maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
