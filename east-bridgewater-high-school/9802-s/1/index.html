East Bridgewater High School is located in East Bridgewater, MA. Students and parents can contact East Bridgewater High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (508) 378-8214. East Bridgewater High School has 668 total students, 307 male, 344 female. Of these students from East Bridgewater High School, 155 are ninth grade students, 160 are tenth grade students, 164 are eleventh grade students, and 189 are twelfth grade students. East Bridgewater High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
