Lakeland High School's Profile

Lakeland High School is located in Shrub Oak, NY. Students and parents can contact Lakeland High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (914) 528-0600. Lakeland High School has 1228 total students, 620 male, 608 female. Of these students from Lakeland High School, 284 are ninth grade students, 282 are tenth grade students, 288 are eleventh grade students, and 323 are twelfth grade students. Lakeland High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
