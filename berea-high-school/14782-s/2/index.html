Berea High School's Profile

Berea High School is located in Berea, OH. Students and parents can contact Berea High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (440) 234-5418. Berea High School has 1200 total students, 596 male, 543 female. Of these students from Berea High School, 289 are ninth grade students, 265 are tenth grade students, 349 are eleventh grade students, and 297 are twelfth grade students. Berea High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
