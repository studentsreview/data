Since all of these reviews are pretty outdated I thought I'd contribute. I take APES with Mr. Rice, so I can't speak for the bio kids, but APES is probably my favorite subject. Mr. Rice is cool, chill, and very funny. His labs are always very interactive, and not very challenging. His exams are a little difficult, but he makes up for it with test corrections. Overall a great teacher and a pleasure to be around. Make sure to sit in the front of the class, otherwise you will have trouble hearing.
