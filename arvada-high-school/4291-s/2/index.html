Arvada High School is located in Arvada, CO. Students and parents can contact Arvada High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (303) 982-0114. Arvada High School has 1364 total students, 719 male, 645 female. Of these students from Arvada High School, 322 are ninth grade students, 399 are tenth grade students, 344 are eleventh grade students, and 299 are twelfth grade students. Arvada High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
