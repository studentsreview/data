Frederick Middle/senior High School is located in Frederick, CO. Students and parents can contact Frederick Middle/senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (303) 833-3533. Frederick Middle/senior High School has 860 total students, 415 male, 445 female. Of these students from Frederick Middle/senior High School, 256 are ninth grade students, 258 are tenth grade students, 198 are eleventh grade students, and 148 are twelfth grade students. Frederick Middle/senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
