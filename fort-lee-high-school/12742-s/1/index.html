Fort Lee High School is located in Fort Lee, NJ. Students and parents can contact Fort Lee High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (201) 585-4675. Fort Lee High School has 1044 total students, 534 male, 510 female. Of these students from Fort Lee High School, 236 are ninth grade students, 287 are tenth grade students, 263 are eleventh grade students, and 258 are twelfth grade students. Fort Lee High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
