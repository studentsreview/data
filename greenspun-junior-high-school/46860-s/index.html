Greenspun Junior High School's Profile

Greenspun Junior High School is located in Henderson, NV. Students and parents can contact Greenspun Junior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (702) 799-2540. Greenspun Junior High School has 1501 total students, 764 male, 737 female. Greenspun Junior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 25:1.
