Hunterdon Central High School is located in Flemington, NJ. Students and parents can contact Hunterdon Central High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (908) 284-7155. Hunterdon Central High School has 3007 total students, 1578 male, 1429 female. Of these students from Hunterdon Central High School, 786 are ninth grade students, 748 are tenth grade students, 699 are eleventh grade students, and 703 are twelfth grade students.
