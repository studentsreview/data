Lakeshore High School is located in Stevensville, MI. Students and parents can contact Lakeshore High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (269) 428-1402. Lakeshore High School has 990 total students, 498 male, 489 female. Of these students from Lakeshore High School, 229 are ninth grade students, 231 are tenth grade students, 257 are eleventh grade students, and 273 are twelfth grade students. Lakeshore High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
