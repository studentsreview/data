Alaska employs over 7,044 teachers who oversee approximately 127,695 students. Students in Alaska submitted more than 55,569 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Alaska is 4.31, which happens to be below the national average of 4.46. Dimond High School, West Anchorage High School, and Ajax High School are schools in Alaska with positive teacher reviews.
