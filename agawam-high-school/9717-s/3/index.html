Agawam High School's Profile

Agawam High School is located in Agawam, MA. Students and parents can contact Agawam High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (413) 821-0521. Agawam High School has 1309 total students, 667 male, 629 female. Of these students from Agawam High School, 370 are ninth grade students, 333 are tenth grade students, 318 are eleventh grade students, and 288 are twelfth grade students. Agawam High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
