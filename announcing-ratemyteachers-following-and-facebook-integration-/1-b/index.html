Announcing RateMyTeachers Following and Facebook Integration.
Adrian Rosebrock, 17th June 2010

I have been working for RateMyTeachers.com for two years now and I will definitely say that 2010 has been the most exciting. In January we released the "following" feature. Users can now follow their favorite teachers and schools, and receive email updates whenever a new teacher is added to a school, a teacher is rated, or a teacher is commented on, all through our dashboard. Following has become extremely popular as of late and it shows what a success the feature is. To start following your favorite teachers and schools either head on over to your dashboard, or go to a teacher/school page and click the follow button. (Note: an account will be required. Click here to register).

Two weeks ago RateMyTeachers integrated with the social networking giant, Facebook. On every school, teacher, and rating page, you can "like" the content on the page and publish it onto your Facebook wall. Don't worry, we do not keep track of your Facebook identify. RateMyTeachers still remains strictly anonymous.

Above all, one thing is certainly clear: we will continue to develop, improve, and create new features to involve our users with content.
