Enterprise- Business And Technology School's Profile

Enterprise- Business And Technology School is located in Brooklyn, NY. Students and parents can contact Enterprise- Business And Technology School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 387-2701. Enterprise- Business And Technology School has 821 total students, 528 male, 293 female. Of these students from Enterprise- Business And Technology School, 258 are ninth grade students, 211 are tenth grade students, 160 are eleventh grade students, and 124 are twelfth grade students. Enterprise- Business And Technology School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
