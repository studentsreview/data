King Philip Regional High School's Profile

King Philip Regional High School is located in Wrentham, MA. Students and parents can contact King Philip Regional High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (978) 355-4651. King Philip Regional High School has 1015 total students, 457 male, 529 female. Of these students from King Philip Regional High School, 285 are ninth grade students, 272 are tenth grade students, 229 are eleventh grade students, and 228 are twelfth grade students. King Philip Regional High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
