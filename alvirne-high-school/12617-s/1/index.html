Alvirne High School's Profile

Alvirne High School is located in Hudson, NH. Students and parents can contact Alvirne High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (603) 886-1260. Alvirne High School has 1498 total students, 769 male, 729 female. Of these students from Alvirne High School, 390 are ninth grade students, 385 are tenth grade students, 389 are eleventh grade students, and 334 are twelfth grade students. Alvirne High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
