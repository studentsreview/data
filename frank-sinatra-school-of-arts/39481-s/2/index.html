Frank Sinatra School Of Arts's Profile

Frank Sinatra School Of Arts is located in Long Island City, NY. Students and parents can contact Frank Sinatra School Of Arts by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 361-9920. Frank Sinatra School Of Arts has 613 total students, 192 male, 421 female. Of these students from Frank Sinatra School Of Arts, 143 are ninth grade students, 182 are tenth grade students, 166 are eleventh grade students, and 122 are twelfth grade students. Frank Sinatra School Of Arts maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
