Jenison High School is located in Jenison, MI. Students and parents can contact Jenison High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (616) 457-3400. Jenison High School has 1634 total students, 798 male, 752 female. Of these students from Jenison High School, 381 are ninth grade students, 437 are tenth grade students, 402 are eleventh grade students, and 414 are twelfth grade students. Jenison High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
