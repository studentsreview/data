Danvers High School's Profile

Danvers High School is located in Danvers, MA. Students and parents can contact Danvers High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (978) 777-8925. Danvers High School has 1010 total students, 432 male, 573 female. Of these students from Danvers High School, 250 are ninth grade students, 269 are tenth grade students, 236 are eleventh grade students, and 255 are twelfth grade students. Danvers High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
