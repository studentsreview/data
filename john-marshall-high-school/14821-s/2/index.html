John Marshall High School's Profile

John Marshall High School is located in Cleveland, OH. Students and parents can contact John Marshall High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (216) 251-5740. John Marshall High School has 1762 total students, 844 male, 878 female. Of these students from John Marshall High School, 600 are ninth grade students, 509 are tenth grade students, 320 are eleventh grade students, and 333 are twelfth grade students. John Marshall High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
