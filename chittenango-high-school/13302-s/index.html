Chittenango High School is located in Chittenango, NY. Students and parents can contact Chittenango High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (315) 687-2902. Chittenango High School has 836 total students, 433 male, 403 female. Of these students from Chittenango High School, 223 are ninth grade students, 201 are tenth grade students, 178 are eleventh grade students, and 225 are twelfth grade students. Chittenango High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
