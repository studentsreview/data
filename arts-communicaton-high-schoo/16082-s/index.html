Arts & Communicaton High Schoo's Profile

Arts & Communicaton High Schoo is located in Beaverton, OR. Students and parents can contact Arts & Communicaton High Schoo by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (503) 672-3700. Arts & Communicaton High Schoo has 305 total students, 100 male, 188 female. Of these students from Arts & Communicaton High Schoo, 91 are ninth grade students, 86 are tenth grade students, 68 are eleventh grade students, and 60 are twelfth grade students. Arts & Communicaton High Schoo maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
