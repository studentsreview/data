Georgia employs over 217,414 teachers who oversee approximately 1,678,482 students. Students in Georgia submitted more than 122,260 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Georgia is 4.55, which happens to be above the national average of 4.46. Hightower Trail Middle School, Savannah Country Day School, and Five Forks Middle School are schools in Georgia with positive teacher reviews.
