  • This is a ridiculously easy class, especially for an AP class. He gives you homework hints so all you do is fill in blanks, and he gives you quiz/test notes, which are just like the actual quiz/test but different numbers. You just have to practice the problems on the notes and you'll ace everything. You will NOT actually learn chemistry through him even if you get an A in his class. He doesn't prep you for the AP exam. He doesn't answer your questions, either. But hey, you can get an easy A.

Geez, he must be teaching some really boring stuff if the teacher himself gets sleepy over it.
