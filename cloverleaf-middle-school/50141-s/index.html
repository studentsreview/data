Cloverleaf Middle School's Profile

Cloverleaf Middle School is located in Lodi, OH. Students and parents can contact Cloverleaf Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (330) 948-2500. Cloverleaf Middle School has 527 total students, 254 male, 269 female. Cloverleaf Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
