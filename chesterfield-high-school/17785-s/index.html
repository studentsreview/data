Chesterfield High School's Profile

Chesterfield High School is located in Chesterfield, SC. Students and parents can contact Chesterfield High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (843) 623-2161. Chesterfield High School has 572 total students, 275 male, 297 female. Of these students from Chesterfield High School, 204 are ninth grade students, 153 are tenth grade students, 111 are eleventh grade students, and 104 are twelfth grade students. Chesterfield High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
