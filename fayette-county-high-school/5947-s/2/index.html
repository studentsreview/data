Fayette County High School's Profile

Fayette County High School is located in Fayetteville, GA. Students and parents can contact Fayette County High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (770) 460-3540. Fayette County High School has 1440 total students, 698 male, 683 female. Of these students from Fayette County High School, 453 are ninth grade students, 330 are tenth grade students, 336 are eleventh grade students, and 321 are twelfth grade students. Fayette County High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
