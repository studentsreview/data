High School Of World Cultures's Profile

High School Of World Cultures is located in New York, NY. Students and parents can contact High School Of World Cultures by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 860-8121. High School Of World Cultures has 299 total students, 144 male, 155 female. Of these students from High School Of World Cultures, 130 are ninth grade students, 37 are tenth grade students, 110 are eleventh grade students, and 22 are twelfth grade students.
