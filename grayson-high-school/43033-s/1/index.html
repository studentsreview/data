Grayson High School's Profile

Grayson High School is located in Loganville, GA. Students and parents can contact Grayson High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (770) 554-1071. Grayson High School has 3230 total students, 1582 male, 1577 female. Of these students from Grayson High School, 915 are ninth grade students, 912 are tenth grade students, 707 are eleventh grade students, and 696 are twelfth grade students. Grayson High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
