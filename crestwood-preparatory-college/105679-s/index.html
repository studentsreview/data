Crestwood Preparatory College's Profile

Crestwood Preparatory College is located in Toronto, CA. Students and parents can contact Crestwood Preparatory College by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (510) 879-1597. Crestwood Preparatory College has 356 total students, 190 male, 166 female. Of these students from Crestwood Preparatory College, 146 are ninth grade students, 90 are tenth grade students, 59 are eleventh grade students, and 61 are twelfth grade students. Crestwood Preparatory College maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
