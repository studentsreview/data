Elwood/john Glenn High School's Profile

Elwood/john Glenn High School is located in Elwood, NY. Students and parents can contact Elwood/john Glenn High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (631) 266-5410. Elwood/john Glenn High School has 805 total students, 416 male, 389 female. Of these students from Elwood/john Glenn High School, 204 are ninth grade students, 184 are tenth grade students, 213 are eleventh grade students, and 204 are twelfth grade students. Elwood/john Glenn High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
