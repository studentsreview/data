Dover High School is located in Dover, OH. Students and parents can contact Dover High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (330) 364-7148. Dover High School has 772 total students, 396 male, 367 female. Of these students from Dover High School, 184 are ninth grade students, 211 are tenth grade students, 178 are eleventh grade students, and 199 are twelfth grade students.
