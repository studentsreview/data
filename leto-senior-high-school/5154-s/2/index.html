Leto Senior High School's Profile

Leto Senior High School is located in Tampa, FL. Students and parents can contact Leto Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (239) 693-5353. Leto Senior High School has 1764 total students, 861 male, 850 female. Of these students from Leto Senior High School, 452 are ninth grade students, 402 are tenth grade students, 422 are eleventh grade students, and 488 are twelfth grade students. Leto Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
