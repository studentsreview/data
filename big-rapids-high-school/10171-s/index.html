Big Rapids High School's Profile

Big Rapids High School is located in Big Rapids, MI. Students and parents can contact Big Rapids High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (231) 796-7651. Big Rapids High School has 670 total students, 356 male, 314 female. Of these students from Big Rapids High School, 156 are ninth grade students, 156 are tenth grade students, 160 are eleventh grade students, and 198 are twelfth grade students. Big Rapids High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
