Hill Regional Career High Scho is located in New Haven, CT. Students and parents can contact Hill Regional Career High Scho by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (203) 946-8400. Hill Regional Career High Scho has 742 total students, 271 male, 471 female. Of these students from Hill Regional Career High Scho, 234 are ninth grade students, 191 are tenth grade students, 162 are eleventh grade students, and 155 are twelfth grade students. Hill Regional Career High Scho maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
