Big Walnut Middle School's Profile

Big Walnut Middle School is located in Sunbury, OH. Students and parents can contact Big Walnut Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (740) 965-3006. Big Walnut Middle School has 670 total students, 331 male, 329 female. Big Walnut Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
