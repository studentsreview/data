Cumberland Valley High School's Profile

Cumberland Valley High School is located in Mechanicsburg, PA. Students and parents can contact Cumberland Valley High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (717) 766-0217. Cumberland Valley High School has 2602 total students, 1359 male, 1243 female. Of these students from Cumberland Valley High School, 648 are ninth grade students, 712 are tenth grade students, 638 are eleventh grade students, and 604 are twelfth grade students. Cumberland Valley High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
