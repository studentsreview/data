Life Sciences Secondary School's Profile

Life Sciences Secondary School is located in New York, NY. Students and parents can contact Life Sciences Secondary School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (212) 348-1694. Life Sciences Secondary School has 709 total students, 326 male, 383 female. Of these students from Life Sciences Secondary School, 166 are ninth grade students, 150 are tenth grade students, 98 are eleventh grade students, and 112 are twelfth grade students. Life Sciences Secondary School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
