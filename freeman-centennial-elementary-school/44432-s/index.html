Freeman-centennial Elementary School's Profile

Freeman-centennial Elementary School is located in Norfolk, MA. Students and parents can contact Freeman-centennial Elementary School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (978) 251-5155. Freeman-centennial Elementary School has 447 total students, 248 male, 192 female. Freeman-centennial Elementary School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
