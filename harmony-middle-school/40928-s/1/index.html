Harmony Middle School's Profile

Harmony Middle School is located in Overland Park, KS. Students and parents can contact Harmony Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (316) 973-7800. Harmony Middle School has 840 total students, 453 male, 387 female. Harmony Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
