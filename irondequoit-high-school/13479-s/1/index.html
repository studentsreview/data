Irondequoit High School is located in Rochester, NY. Students and parents can contact Irondequoit High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (585) 266-7351. Irondequoit High School has 1412 total students, 697 male, 715 female. Of these students from Irondequoit High School, 366 are ninth grade students, 334 are tenth grade students, 345 are eleventh grade students, and 367 are twelfth grade students. Irondequoit High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
