Douglas Middle High School is located in East Douglas, MA. Students and parents can contact Douglas Middle High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (508) 476-4100. Douglas Middle High School has 590 total students, 300 male, 288 female. Of these students from Douglas Middle High School, 116 are ninth grade students, 98 are tenth grade students, 120 are eleventh grade students, and 107 are twelfth grade students. Douglas Middle High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
