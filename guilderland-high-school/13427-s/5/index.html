Guilderland High School's Profile

Guilderland High School is located in Guilderland Center, NY. Students and parents can contact Guilderland High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (518) 861-8591. Guilderland High School has 1903 total students, 903 male, 1000 female. Of these students from Guilderland High School, 489 are ninth grade students, 468 are tenth grade students, 450 are eleventh grade students, and 479 are twelfth grade students. Guilderland High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
