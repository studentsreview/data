Beaufort High School's Profile

Beaufort High School is located in Beaufort, SC. Students and parents can contact Beaufort High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (843) 322-2000. Beaufort High School has 1604 total students, 773 male, 830 female. Of these students from Beaufort High School, 443 are ninth grade students, 419 are tenth grade students, 408 are eleventh grade students, and 334 are twelfth grade students. Beaufort High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
