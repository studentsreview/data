Beecher High School is located in Beecher, IL. Students and parents can contact Beecher High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (708) 946-2266. Beecher High School has 393 total students, 186 male, 200 female. Of these students from Beecher High School, 113 are ninth grade students, 91 are tenth grade students, 99 are eleventh grade students, and 90 are twelfth grade students. Beecher High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
