Langley High School's Profile

Langley High School is located in Mclean, VA. Students and parents can contact Langley High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (703) 287-2700. Langley High School has 2100 total students, 1081 male, 942 female. Of these students from Langley High School, 525 are ninth grade students, 542 are tenth grade students, 532 are eleventh grade students, and 501 are twelfth grade students. Langley High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
