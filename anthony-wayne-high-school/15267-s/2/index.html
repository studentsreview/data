Anthony Wayne High School's Profile

Anthony Wayne High School is located in Whitehouse, OH. Students and parents can contact Anthony Wayne High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (419) 877-0466. Anthony Wayne High School has 1342 total students, 653 male, 679 female. Of these students from Anthony Wayne High School, 325 are ninth grade students, 343 are tenth grade students, 342 are eleventh grade students, and 332 are twelfth grade students. Anthony Wayne High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
