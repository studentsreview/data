Herbert H. Lehman High School's Profile

Herbert H. Lehman High School is located in Bronx, NY. Students and parents can contact Herbert H. Lehman High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 904-4200. Herbert H. Lehman High School has 4322 total students, 2525 male, 1797 female. Of these students from Herbert H. Lehman High School, 1661 are ninth grade students, 927 are tenth grade students, 646 are eleventh grade students, and 519 are twelfth grade students. Herbert H. Lehman High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
