Birmingham High School's Profile

Birmingham High School is located in Lake Balboa, CA. Students and parents can contact Birmingham High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (818) 758-5200. Birmingham High School has 3375 total students, 1701 male, 1674 female. Of these students from Birmingham High School, 1012 are ninth grade students, 943 are tenth grade students, 797 are eleventh grade students, and 623 are twelfth grade students. Birmingham High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
