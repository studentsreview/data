Henry M Jackson High School's Profile

Henry M Jackson High School is located in Mill Creek, WA. Students and parents can contact Henry M Jackson High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (425) 385-7000. Henry M Jackson High School has 1896 total students, 966 male, 896 female. Of these students from Henry M Jackson High School, 544 are ninth grade students, 518 are tenth grade students, 468 are eleventh grade students, and 366 are twelfth grade students. Henry M Jackson High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
