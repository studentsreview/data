Brandywine Jr-sr High School's Profile

Brandywine Jr-sr High School is located in Niles, MI. Students and parents can contact Brandywine Jr-sr High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (269) 683-4800. Brandywine Jr-sr High School has 457 total students, 255 male, 202 female. Of these students from Brandywine Jr-sr High School, 126 are ninth grade students, 116 are tenth grade students, 106 are eleventh grade students, and 109 are twelfth grade students. Brandywine Jr-sr High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
