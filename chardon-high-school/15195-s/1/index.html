Chardon High School's Profile

Chardon High School is located in Chardon, OH. Students and parents can contact Chardon High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (440) 285-4057. Chardon High School has 1132 total students, 552 male, 576 female. Of these students from Chardon High School, 301 are ninth grade students, 278 are tenth grade students, 257 are eleventh grade students, and 296 are twelfth grade students. Chardon High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
