Only one-third of high school freshmen can read!

Leave a reply

47% of high school kids can read, but only one third of  them are reading at grade level. Most of this students struggled with this challenge during their first year of elementary school. Some of them have to find solutions (teachers and special programs) after school to help them overcome this issue while they are young.
