Bassett Senior High School is located in La Puente, CA. Students and parents can contact Bassett Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (626) 931-2800. Bassett Senior High School has 1380 total students, 654 male, 726 female. Of these students from Bassett Senior High School, 366 are ninth grade students, 354 are tenth grade students, 338 are eleventh grade students, and 322 are twelfth grade students. Bassett Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
