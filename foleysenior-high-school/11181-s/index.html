Foleysenior High School's Profile

Foleysenior High School is located in Foley, MN. Students and parents can contact Foleysenior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (320) 968-7246. Foleysenior High School has 517 total students, 266 male, 251 female. Of these students from Foleysenior High School, 127 are ninth grade students, 138 are tenth grade students, 125 are eleventh grade students, and 127 are twelfth grade students. Foleysenior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
