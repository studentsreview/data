Buckhann Upshur High School's Profile

Buckhann Upshur High School is located in Buckhannon, WV. Students and parents can contact Buckhann Upshur High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (304) 304-3720. Buckhann Upshur High School has 1171 total students, 614 male, 557 female.
