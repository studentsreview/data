Briarcliff High School is located in Briarcliff Manor, NY. Students and parents can contact Briarcliff High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (914) 769-6299. Briarcliff High School has 642 total students, 335 male, 307 female. Of these students from Briarcliff High School, 149 are ninth grade students, 160 are tenth grade students, 180 are eleventh grade students, and 149 are twelfth grade students. Briarcliff High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
