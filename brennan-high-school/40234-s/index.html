Brennan High School is located in North Babylon, NY. Students and parents can contact Brennan High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (631) 434-2204. Brennan High School has 3605 total students, 1875 male, 1730 female. Of these students from Brennan High School, 133 are ninth grade students, 1369 are tenth grade students, 1217 are eleventh grade students, and 886 are twelfth grade students. Brennan High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
