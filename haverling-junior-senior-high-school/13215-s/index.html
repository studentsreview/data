Haverling Junior Senior High School is located in Bath, NY. Students and parents can contact Haverling Junior Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (607) 776-4107. Haverling Junior Senior High School has 597 total students, 308 male, 289 female. Of these students from Haverling Junior Senior High School, 165 are ninth grade students, 154 are tenth grade students, 128 are eleventh grade students, and 141 are twelfth grade students. Haverling Junior Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
