La Serna High School's Profile

La Serna High School is located in Whittier, CA. Students and parents can contact La Serna High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (562) 698-8121. La Serna High School has 2382 total students, 1185 male, 1197 female. Of these students from La Serna High School, 569 are ninth grade students, 660 are tenth grade students, 603 are eleventh grade students, and 550 are twelfth grade students. La Serna High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
