Eldorado High School's Profile

Eldorado High School is located in Las Vegas, NV. Students and parents can contact Eldorado High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (702) 799-7200. Eldorado High School has 3153 total students, 1593 male, 1560 female. Of these students from Eldorado High School, 1251 are ninth grade students, 897 are tenth grade students, 538 are eleventh grade students, and 458 are twelfth grade students. Eldorado High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
