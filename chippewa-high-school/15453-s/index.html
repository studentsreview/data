Chippewa High School's Profile

Chippewa High School is located in Doylestown, OH. Students and parents can contact Chippewa High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (330) 658-2011. Chippewa High School has 515 total students, 257 male, 256 female. Of these students from Chippewa High School, 116 are ninth grade students, 141 are tenth grade students, 128 are eleventh grade students, and 128 are twelfth grade students. Chippewa High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 29:1.
