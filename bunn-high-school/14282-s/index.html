Bunn High School's Profile

Bunn High School is located in Bunn, NC. Students and parents can contact Bunn High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (704) 482-5354. Bunn High School has 1329 total students, 661 male, 650 female. Of these students from Bunn High School, 404 are ninth grade students, 353 are tenth grade students, 273 are eleventh grade students, and 299 are twelfth grade students. Bunn High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
