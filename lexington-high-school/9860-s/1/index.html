Lexington High School's Profile

Lexington High School is located in Lexington, MA. Students and parents can contact Lexington High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (781) 861-2320. Lexington High School has 1995 total students, 978 male, 981 female. Of these students from Lexington High School, 502 are ninth grade students, 471 are tenth grade students, 512 are eleventh grade students, and 510 are twelfth grade students. Lexington High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
