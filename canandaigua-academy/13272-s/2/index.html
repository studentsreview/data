Canandaigua Academy is located in Canandaigua, NY. Students and parents can contact Canandaigua Academy by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (585) 396-3800. Canandaigua Academy has 1366 total students, 709 male, 657 female. Of these students from Canandaigua Academy, 327 are ninth grade students, 355 are tenth grade students, 357 are eleventh grade students, and 318 are twelfth grade students. Canandaigua Academy maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
