Linwood Middle School's Profile

Linwood Middle School is located in North Brunswick, NJ. Students and parents can contact Linwood Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (856) 346-3330. Linwood Middle School has 649 total students, 331 male, 318 female. Linwood Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
