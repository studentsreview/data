Arkansas employs over 4,121 teachers who oversee approximately 474,706 students. Students in Arkansas submitted more than 7,395 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Arkansas is 4.43, which happens to be below the national average of 4.46. Bentonville High School, Bryant High School, and Fayetteville High School are schools in Arkansas with positive teacher reviews.
