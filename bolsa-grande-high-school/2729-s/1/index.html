Bolsa Grande High School's Profile

Bolsa Grande High School is located in Garden Grove, CA. Students and parents can contact Bolsa Grande High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (714) 663-6424. Bolsa Grande High School has 1774 total students, 902 male, 872 female. Of these students from Bolsa Grande High School, 522 are ninth grade students, 433 are tenth grade students, 430 are eleventh grade students, and 389 are twelfth grade students. Bolsa Grande High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
