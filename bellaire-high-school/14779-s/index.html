Bellaire High School's Profile

Bellaire High School is located in Bellaire, OH. Students and parents can contact Bellaire High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (740) 676-3652. Bellaire High School has 481 total students, 252 male, 216 female. Of these students from Bellaire High School, 117 are ninth grade students, 120 are tenth grade students, 120 are eleventh grade students, and 124 are twelfth grade students. Bellaire High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
