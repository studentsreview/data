Kennesaw Mountain High School's Profile

Kennesaw Mountain High School is located in Kennesaw, GA. Students and parents can contact Kennesaw Mountain High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (770) 426-3300. Kennesaw Mountain High School has 2967 total students, 1453 male, 1428 female. Of these students from Kennesaw Mountain High School, 743 are ninth grade students, 734 are tenth grade students, 742 are eleventh grade students, and 748 are twelfth grade students. Kennesaw Mountain High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
