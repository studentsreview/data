Hillel Community High School's Profile

Hillel Community High School is located in North Miami Beach, FL. Students and parents can contact Hillel Community High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (561) 540-2400. Hillel Community High School has 1957 total students, 999 male, 906 female. Of these students from Hillel Community High School, 594 are ninth grade students, 533 are tenth grade students, 474 are eleventh grade students, and 356 are twelfth grade students. Hillel Community High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
