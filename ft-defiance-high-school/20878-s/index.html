Ft. Defiance High School's Profile

Ft. Defiance High School is located in Fort Defiance, VA. Students and parents can contact Ft. Defiance High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (540) 245-5050. Ft. Defiance High School has 863 total students, 451 male, 412 female. Of these students from Ft. Defiance High School, 243 are ninth grade students, 206 are tenth grade students, 223 are eleventh grade students, and 191 are twelfth grade students. Ft. Defiance High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
