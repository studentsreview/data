Garfield High School is located in Garfield, NJ. Students and parents can contact Garfield High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (973) 340-5010. Garfield High School has 1142 total students, 561 male, 581 female. Of these students from Garfield High School, 307 are ninth grade students, 240 are tenth grade students, 259 are eleventh grade students, and 278 are twelfth grade students. Garfield High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
