Fishers High School's Profile

Fishers High School is located in Fishers, IN. Students and parents can contact Fishers High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (317) 915-4290. Fishers High School has 1942 total students, 972 male, 894 female. Of these students from Fishers High School, 531 are ninth grade students, 502 are tenth grade students, 487 are eleventh grade students, and 422 are twelfth grade students. Fishers High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
