Beaverton High School's Profile

Beaverton High School is located in Beaverton, OR. Students and parents can contact Beaverton High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (503) 259-5000. Beaverton High School has 2046 total students, 1024 male, 922 female. Of these students from Beaverton High School, 501 are ninth grade students, 497 are tenth grade students, 547 are eleventh grade students, and 498 are twelfth grade students. Beaverton High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
