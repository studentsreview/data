East Rockaway Junior Senior High School's Profile

East Rockaway Junior Senior High School is located in East Rockaway, NY. Students and parents can contact East Rockaway Junior Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (516) 887-8300. East Rockaway Junior Senior High School has 596 total students, 310 male, 286 female. Of these students from East Rockaway Junior Senior High School, 111 are ninth grade students, 108 are tenth grade students, 87 are eleventh grade students, and 98 are twelfth grade students. East Rockaway Junior Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
