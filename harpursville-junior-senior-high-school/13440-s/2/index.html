Harpursville Junior Senior High School is located in Harpursville, NY. Students and parents can contact Harpursville Junior Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (607) 693-8105. Harpursville Junior Senior High School has 455 total students, 236 male, 218 female. Of these students from Harpursville Junior Senior High School, 93 are ninth grade students, 65 are tenth grade students, 86 are eleventh grade students, and 74 are twelfth grade students.
