Kellogg Middle School is located in Portland, OR. Students and parents can contact Kellogg Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (541) 687-3224. Kellogg Middle School has 483 total students, 232 male, 208 female. Kellogg Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
