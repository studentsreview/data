Cherry Hill High School (east)'s Profile

Cherry Hill High School (east) is located in Cherry Hill, NJ. Students and parents can contact Cherry Hill High School (east) by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (856) 424-2222. Cherry Hill High School (east) has 2187 total students, 1159 male, 1028 female. Of these students from Cherry Hill High School (east), 552 are ninth grade students, 543 are tenth grade students, 559 are eleventh grade students, and 531 are twelfth grade students. Cherry Hill High School (east) maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
