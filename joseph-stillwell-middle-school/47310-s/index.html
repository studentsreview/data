Joseph Stillwell Middle School is located in Jacksonville, FL. Students and parents can contact Joseph Stillwell Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (904) 693-7523. Joseph Stillwell Middle School has 1225 total students, 577 male, 602 female. Joseph Stillwell Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
