Boys & Girls High School is located in Brooklyn, NY. Students and parents can contact Boys & Girls High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 467-1700. Boys & Girls High School has 3710 total students, 2177 male, 1533 female. Of these students from Boys & Girls High School, 534 are ninth grade students, 554 are tenth grade students, 798 are eleventh grade students, and 1291 are twelfth grade students.
