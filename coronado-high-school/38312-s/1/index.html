Coronado High School is located in Henderson, NV. Students and parents can contact Coronado High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (702) 799-6800. Coronado High School has 3017 total students, 1583 male, 1434 female. Of these students from Coronado High School, 836 are ninth grade students, 860 are tenth grade students, 684 are eleventh grade students, and 634 are twelfth grade students. Coronado High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 27:1.
