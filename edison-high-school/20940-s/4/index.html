Edison High School's Profile

Edison High School is located in Alexandria, VA. Students and parents can contact Edison High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (703) 924-8000. Edison High School has 1806 total students, 880 male, 841 female. Of these students from Edison High School, 457 are ninth grade students, 441 are tenth grade students, 450 are eleventh grade students, and 458 are twelfth grade students. Edison High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
