Edison High School is located in Fresno, CA. Students and parents can contact Edison High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (714) 962-1356. Edison High School has 2590 total students, 1349 male, 1241 female. Of these students from Edison High School, 634 are ninth grade students, 637 are tenth grade students, 600 are eleventh grade students, and 570 are twelfth grade students. Edison High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
