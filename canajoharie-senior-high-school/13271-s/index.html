Canajoharie Senior High School is located in Canajoharie, NY. Students and parents can contact Canajoharie Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (518) 673-6330. Canajoharie Senior High School has 363 total students, 178 male, 185 female. Of these students from Canajoharie Senior High School, 91 are ninth grade students, 81 are tenth grade students, 86 are eleventh grade students, and 105 are twelfth grade students. Canajoharie Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
