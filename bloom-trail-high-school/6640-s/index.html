Bloom Trail High School is located in Chicago Heights, IL. Students and parents can contact Bloom Trail High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (708) 758-7000. Bloom Trail High School has 1603 total students, 768 male, 776 female. Of these students from Bloom Trail High School, 600 are ninth grade students, 414 are tenth grade students, 303 are eleventh grade students, and 286 are twelfth grade students. Bloom Trail High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 25:1.
