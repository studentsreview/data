Clarkston Junior High School's Profile

Clarkston Junior High School is located in Clarkston, MI. Students and parents can contact Clarkston Junior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (248) 623-5600. Clarkston Junior High School has 1304 total students, 636 male, 625 female. Clarkston Junior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
