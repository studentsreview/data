John Dewey High School is located in Brooklyn, NY. Students and parents can contact John Dewey High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 373-6400. John Dewey High School has 3240 total students, 1542 male, 1698 female. Of these students from John Dewey High School, 1214 are ninth grade students, 900 are tenth grade students, 517 are eleventh grade students, and 384 are twelfth grade students. John Dewey High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
