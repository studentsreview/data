Dumont High School's Profile

Dumont High School is located in Dumont, NJ. Students and parents can contact Dumont High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (201) 387-3060. Dumont High School has 883 total students, 432 male, 451 female. Of these students from Dumont High School, 226 are ninth grade students, 238 are tenth grade students, 210 are eleventh grade students, and 209 are twelfth grade students. Dumont High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
