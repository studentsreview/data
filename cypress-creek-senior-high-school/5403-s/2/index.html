Cypress Creek Senior High School's Profile

Cypress Creek Senior High School is located in Orlando, FL. Students and parents can contact Cypress Creek Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (407) 852-3400. Cypress Creek Senior High School has 3399 total students, 1676 male, 1694 female. Of these students from Cypress Creek Senior High School, 979 are ninth grade students, 900 are tenth grade students, 894 are eleventh grade students, and 626 are twelfth grade students. Cypress Creek Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
