Lo-ellen Secondary School's Profile

Lo-ellen Secondary School is located in Sudbury, AK. Students and parents can contact Lo-ellen Secondary School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (907) 742-4950. Lo-ellen Secondary School has 283 total students, 120 male, 135 female. Of these students from Lo-ellen Secondary School, 50 are ninth grade students, 47 are tenth grade students, 46 are eleventh grade students, and 39 are twelfth grade students. Lo-ellen Secondary School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
