Coginchaug Regional High Schoo is located in Durham, CT. Students and parents can contact Coginchaug Regional High Schoo by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (860) 349-7215. Coginchaug Regional High Schoo has 587 total students, 289 male, 298 female. Of these students from Coginchaug Regional High Schoo, 146 are ninth grade students, 159 are tenth grade students, 134 are eleventh grade students, and 148 are twelfth grade students. Coginchaug Regional High Schoo maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
