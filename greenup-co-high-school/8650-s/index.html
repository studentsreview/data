Greenup Co High School is located in Greenup, KY. Students and parents can contact Greenup Co High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (606) 473-9812. Greenup Co High School has 986 total students, 501 male, 484 female. Of these students from Greenup Co High School, 290 are ninth grade students, 266 are tenth grade students, 237 are eleventh grade students, and 193 are twelfth grade students. Greenup Co High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
