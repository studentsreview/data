Charlotte Valley School's Profile

Charlotte Valley School is located in Davenport, NY. Students and parents can contact Charlotte Valley School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (607) 278-5511. Charlotte Valley School has 410 total students, 203 male, 207 female. Of these students from Charlotte Valley School, 34 are ninth grade students, 39 are tenth grade students, 24 are eleventh grade students, and 30 are twelfth grade students. Charlotte Valley School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 10:1.
