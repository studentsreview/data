Alternative Community School is located in Ithaca, NY. Students and parents can contact Alternative Community School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (607) 274-2183. Alternative Community School has 262 total students, 129 male, 133 female. Of these students from Alternative Community School, 39 are ninth grade students, 41 are tenth grade students, 37 are eleventh grade students, and 30 are twelfth grade students. Alternative Community School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 11:1.
