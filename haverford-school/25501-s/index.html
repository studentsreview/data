Haverford School's Profile

Haverford School is located in Haverford, PA. Students and parents can contact Haverford School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (610) 853-5900. Haverford School has 1831 total students, 885 male, 946 female. Of these students from Haverford School, 477 are ninth grade students, 453 are tenth grade students, 421 are eleventh grade students, and 480 are twelfth grade students. Haverford School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
