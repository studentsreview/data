John Bowne High School is located in Flushing, NY. Students and parents can contact John Bowne High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 263-1919. John Bowne High School has 3060 total students, 1493 male, 1567 female. Of these students from John Bowne High School, 1125 are ninth grade students, 721 are tenth grade students, 472 are eleventh grade students, and 532 are twelfth grade students. John Bowne High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
