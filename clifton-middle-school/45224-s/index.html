Clifton Middle School's Profile

Clifton Middle School is located in Houston, TX. Students and parents can contact Clifton Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (817) 408-4800. Clifton Middle School has 777 total students, 425 male, 352 female. Clifton Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
