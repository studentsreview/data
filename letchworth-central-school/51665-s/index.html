Letchworth Central School's Profile

Letchworth Central School is located in Letchworth, NY. Students and parents can contact Letchworth Central School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (518) 494-3015. Letchworth Central School has 578 total students, 310 male, 268 female. Of these students from Letchworth Central School, 45 are ninth grade students, 49 are tenth grade students, 43 are eleventh grade students, and 50 are twelfth grade students. Letchworth Central School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 9:1.
