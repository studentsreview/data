Buchanan High School's Profile

Buchanan High School is located in Clovis, CA. Students and parents can contact Buchanan High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (559) 327-3000. Buchanan High School has 2938 total students, 1481 male, 1457 female. Of these students from Buchanan High School, 584 are ninth grade students, 843 are tenth grade students, 820 are eleventh grade students, and 691 are twelfth grade students. Buchanan High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
