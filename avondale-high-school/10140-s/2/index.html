Avondale High School's Profile

Avondale High School is located in Auburn Hills, MI. Students and parents can contact Avondale High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (248) 537-6100. Avondale High School has 1130 total students, 560 male, 559 female. Of these students from Avondale High School, 326 are ninth grade students, 300 are tenth grade students, 281 are eleventh grade students, and 223 are twelfth grade students. Avondale High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
