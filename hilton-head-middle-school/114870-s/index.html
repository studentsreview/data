Hilton Head Middle School's Profile

Hilton Head Middle School is located in Hilton Head Island, SC. Students and parents can contact Hilton Head Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (843) 689-4500. Hilton Head Middle School has 900 total students, 461 male, 439 female. Hilton Head Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
