Clear Spring High School's Profile

Clear Spring High School is located in Clear Spring, MD. Students and parents can contact Clear Spring High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (301) 766-8082. Clear Spring High School has 547 total students, 297 male, 250 female. Of these students from Clear Spring High School, 137 are ninth grade students, 150 are tenth grade students, 135 are eleventh grade students, and 125 are twelfth grade students. Clear Spring High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
