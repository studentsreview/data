East Windsor Senior High School is located in East Windsor, CT. Students and parents can contact East Windsor Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (860) 623-3361. East Windsor Senior High School has 454 total students, 230 male, 224 female. Of these students from East Windsor Senior High School, 105 are ninth grade students, 119 are tenth grade students, 111 are eleventh grade students, and 119 are twelfth grade students.
