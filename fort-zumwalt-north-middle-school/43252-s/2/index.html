Fort Zumwalt North Middle School's Profile

Fort Zumwalt North Middle School is located in O'fallon, MO. Students and parents can contact Fort Zumwalt North Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (636) 281-2356. Fort Zumwalt North Middle School has 1130 total students, 554 male, 576 female. Fort Zumwalt North Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
