Lakewood High School's Profile

Lakewood High School is located in St. Petersburg, FL. Students and parents can contact Lakewood High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (727) 893-2916. Lakewood High School has 1651 total students, 952 male, 623 female. Of these students from Lakewood High School, 443 are ninth grade students, 396 are tenth grade students, 508 are eleventh grade students, and 304 are twelfth grade students. Lakewood High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
