Illinois employs over 225,291 teachers who oversee approximately 1,968,950 students. Students in Illinois submitted more than 377,775 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Illinois is 4.51, which happens to be above the national average of 4.46. Stillman Valley High School, Oak Lawn Community High School, and George B. Armstrong International Studies Elementary School are schools in Illinois with positive teacher reviews.
