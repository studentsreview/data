Camden Senior High School is located in Camden, NY. Students and parents can contact Camden Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (315) 245-3168. Camden Senior High School has 865 total students, 423 male, 442 female. Of these students from Camden Senior High School, 253 are ninth grade students, 202 are tenth grade students, 197 are eleventh grade students, and 213 are twelfth grade students. Camden Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
