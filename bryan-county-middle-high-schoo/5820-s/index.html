Bryan County Middle/high Schoo's Profile

Bryan County Middle/high Schoo is located in Pembroke, GA. Students and parents can contact Bryan County Middle/high Schoo by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (229) 849-2765. Bryan County Middle/high Schoo has 349 total students, 177 male, 170 female.
