Laquinta High School's Profile

Laquinta High School is located in Westminster, CA. Students and parents can contact Laquinta High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (760) 772-4150. Laquinta High School has 2878 total students, 1478 male, 1400 female. Of these students from Laquinta High School, 840 are ninth grade students, 798 are tenth grade students, 690 are eleventh grade students, and 550 are twelfth grade students. Laquinta High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
