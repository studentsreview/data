Francis T. Maloney High School's Profile

Francis T. Maloney High School is located in Meriden, CT. Students and parents can contact Francis T. Maloney High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (203) 238-2334. Francis T. Maloney High School has 1322 total students, 677 male, 645 female. Of these students from Francis T. Maloney High School, 397 are ninth grade students, 317 are tenth grade students, 306 are eleventh grade students, and 302 are twelfth grade students. Francis T. Maloney High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
