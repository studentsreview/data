Granada High School's Profile

Granada High School is located in Livermore, CA. Students and parents can contact Granada High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (925) 606-4800. Granada High School has 2303 total students, 1162 male, 1141 female. Of these students from Granada High School, 631 are ninth grade students, 593 are tenth grade students, 550 are eleventh grade students, and 529 are twelfth grade students. Granada High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
