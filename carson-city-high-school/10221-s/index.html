Carson City High School

Carson City High School is located in Carson City, MI. Students and parents can contact Carson City High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (989) 584-3175. Carson City High School has 416 total students, 210 male, 206 female. Of these students from Carson City High School, 114 are ninth grade students, 120 are tenth grade students, 98 are eleventh grade students, and 84 are twelfth grade students. Carson City High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
