Kenowa Hills High School is located in Grand Rapids, MI. Students and parents can contact Kenowa Hills High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (616) 784-2400. Kenowa Hills High School has 1249 total students, 629 male, 616 female. Of these students from Kenowa Hills High School, 315 are ninth grade students, 312 are tenth grade students, 326 are eleventh grade students, and 296 are twelfth grade students. Kenowa Hills High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
