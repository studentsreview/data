Arthur P. Schalick High School is located in Pittsgrove, NJ. Students and parents can contact Arthur P. Schalick High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (856) 358-2054. Arthur P. Schalick High School has 597 total students, 318 male, 279 female. Of these students from Arthur P.
