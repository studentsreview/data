Lindale High School's Profile

Lindale High School is located in Lindale, TX. Students and parents can contact Lindale High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (281) 448-5231. Lindale High School has 2380 total students, 1256 male, 1124 female. Of these students from Lindale High School, 175 are ninth grade students, 994 are tenth grade students, 566 are eleventh grade students, and 645 are twelfth grade students. Lindale High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
