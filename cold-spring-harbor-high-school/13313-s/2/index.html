Cold Spring Harbor High School's Profile

Cold Spring Harbor High School is located in Cold Spring Harbor, NY. Students and parents can contact Cold Spring Harbor High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (631) 367-6900. Cold Spring Harbor High School has 1040 total students, 530 male, 510 female. Of these students from Cold Spring Harbor High School, 176 are ninth grade students, 167 are tenth grade students, 172 are eleventh grade students, and 177 are twelfth grade students. Cold Spring Harbor High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
