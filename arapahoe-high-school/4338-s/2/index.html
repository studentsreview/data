Arapahoe High School is located in Littleton, CO. Students and parents can contact Arapahoe High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (303) 347-6000. Arapahoe High School has 2220 total students, 1115 male, 1105 female. Arapahoe High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
