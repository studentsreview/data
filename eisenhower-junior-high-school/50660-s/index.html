Eisenhower Junior High School's Profile

Eisenhower Junior High School is located in Hoffman Estates, IL. Students and parents can contact Eisenhower Junior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (630) 964-5200. Eisenhower Junior High School has 567 total students, 283 male, 274 female. Eisenhower Junior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
