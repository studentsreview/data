Clementon Middle School's Profile

Clementon Middle School is located in Clementon, NJ. Students and parents can contact Clementon Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (973) 335-9700. Clementon Middle School has 165 total students, 89 male, 76 female. Clementon Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
