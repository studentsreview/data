Chester Junior Senior High School is located in Chester, NY. Students and parents can contact Chester Junior Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (914) 793-6130. Chester Junior Senior High School has 878 total students, 447 male, 431 female. Of these students from Chester Junior Senior High School, 212 are ninth grade students, 237 are tenth grade students, 206 are eleventh grade students, and 203 are twelfth grade students. Chester Junior Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
