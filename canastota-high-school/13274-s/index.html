Canastota High School is located in Canastota, NY. Students and parents can contact Canastota High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (315) 697-2003. Canastota High School has 756 total students, 368 male, 388 female. Of these students from Canastota High School, 140 are ninth grade students, 116 are tenth grade students, 119 are eleventh grade students, and 136 are twelfth grade students. Canastota High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
