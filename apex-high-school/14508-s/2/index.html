Apex High School is located in Apex, NC. Students and parents can contact Apex High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (704) 482-5354. Apex High School has 1329 total students, 661 male, 650 female. Of these students from Apex High School, 404 are ninth grade students, 353 are tenth grade students, 273 are eleventh grade students, and 299 are twelfth grade students. Apex High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
