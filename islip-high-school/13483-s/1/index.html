Islip High School is located in Islip, NY. Students and parents can contact Islip High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (631) 859-2234. Islip High School has 1154 total students, 601 male, 553 female. Of these students from Islip High School, 299 are ninth grade students, 311 are tenth grade students, 269 are eleventh grade students, and 274 are twelfth grade students. Islip High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
