Grand Blanc Community High School is located in Grand Blanc, MI. Students and parents can contact Grand Blanc Community High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (810) 591-6638. Grand Blanc Community High School has 2528 total students, 1232 male, 1203 female. Of these students from Grand Blanc Community High School, 645 are ninth grade students, 687 are tenth grade students, 662 are eleventh grade students, and 534 are twelfth grade students.
