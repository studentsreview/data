Bogota High School is located in Bogota, NJ. Students and parents can contact Bogota High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (973) 729-6191. Bogota High School has 1235 total students, 649 male, 586 female. Of these students from Bogota High School, 314 are ninth grade students, 309 are tenth grade students, 327 are eleventh grade students, and 272 are twelfth grade students. Bogota High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
