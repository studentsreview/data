Emerson High School's Profile

Emerson High School is located in Union City, NJ. Students and parents can contact Emerson High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (201) 348-5901. Emerson High School has 1377 total students, 706 male, 671 female. Of these students from Emerson High School, 388 are ninth grade students, 345 are tenth grade students, 285 are eleventh grade students, and 317 are twelfth grade students. Emerson High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
