Bristol Central High School is located in Bristol, CT. Students and parents can contact Bristol Central High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (860) 584-7732. Bristol Central High School has 1465 total students, 768 male, 697 female. Of these students from Bristol Central High School, 413 are ninth grade students, 323 are tenth grade students, 369 are eleventh grade students, and 360 are twelfth grade students. Bristol Central High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
