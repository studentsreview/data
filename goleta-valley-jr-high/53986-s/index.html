Goleta Valley Jr High's Profile

Goleta Valley Jr High is located in Goleta, CA. Students and parents can contact Goleta Valley Jr High by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (805) 967-3486. Goleta Valley Jr High has 776 total students, 394 male, 382 female. Goleta Valley Jr High maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
