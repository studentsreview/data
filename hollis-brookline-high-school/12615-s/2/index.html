Hollis/brookline High School's Profile

Hollis/brookline High School is located in Hollis, NH. Students and parents can contact Hollis/brookline High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (603) 465-2269. Hollis/brookline High School has 929 total students, 490 male, 439 female. Of these students from Hollis/brookline High School, 242 are ninth grade students, 239 are tenth grade students, 221 are eleventh grade students, and 227 are twelfth grade students. Hollis/brookline High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
