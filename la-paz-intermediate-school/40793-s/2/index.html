La Paz Intermediate School is located in Mission Viejo, CA. Students and parents can contact La Paz Intermediate School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (949) 830-1720. La Paz Intermediate School has 1112 total students, 603 male, 509 female. La Paz Intermediate School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
