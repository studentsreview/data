Frontier Trail Junior High's Profile

Frontier Trail Junior High is located in Olathe, KS. Students and parents can contact Frontier Trail Junior High by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (913) 780-7210. Frontier Trail Junior High has 710 total students, 333 male, 359 female. Frontier Trail Junior High maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
