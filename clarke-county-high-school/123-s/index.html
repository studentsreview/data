Clarke County High School is located in Grove Hill, AL. Students and parents can contact Clarke County High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (334) 275-3368. Clarke County High School has 422 total students, 209 male, 213 female. Of these students from Clarke County High School, 122 are ninth grade students, 118 are tenth grade students, 97 are eleventh grade students, and 85 are twelfth grade students. Clarke County High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
