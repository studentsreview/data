Carrabec High School is located in N Anson, ME. Students and parents can contact Carrabec High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (207) 635-2296. Carrabec High School has 263 total students, 134 male, 129 female. Of these students from Carrabec High School, 75 are ninth grade students, 80 are tenth grade students, 60 are eleventh grade students, and 48 are twelfth grade students. Carrabec High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 9:1.
