John F Kennedy Catholic High School is located in Somers, NY. Students and parents can contact John F Kennedy Catholic High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 817-7400. John F Kennedy Catholic High School has 2090 total students, 1295 male, 795 female. Of these students from John F Kennedy Catholic High School, 666 are ninth grade students, 402 are tenth grade students, 395 are eleventh grade students, and 310 are twelfth grade students. John F Kennedy Catholic High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
