John F Kennedy Catholic High School is located in Somers, NY. Students and parents can contact John F Kennedy Catholic High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 817-7400. John F Kennedy Catholic High School has 2090 total students, 1295 male, 795 female.
