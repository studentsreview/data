Cleveland (grover) High School's Profile

Cleveland (grover) High School is located in Reseda, CA. Students and parents can contact Cleveland (grover) High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (818) 885-2300. Cleveland (grover) High School has 3768 total students, 1841 male, 1927 female. Of these students from Cleveland (grover) High School, 1135 are ninth grade students, 1060 are tenth grade students, 880 are eleventh grade students, and 693 are twelfth grade students. Cleveland (grover) High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
