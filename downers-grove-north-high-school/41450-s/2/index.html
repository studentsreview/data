Downers Grove North High School's Profile

Downers Grove North High School is located in Downers Grove, IL. Students and parents can contact Downers Grove North High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (217) 374-2131. Downers Grove North High School has 356 total students, 165 male, 190 female. Of these students from Downers Grove North High School, 91 are ninth grade students, 88 are tenth grade students, 76 are eleventh grade students, and 101 are twelfth grade students. Downers Grove North High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
