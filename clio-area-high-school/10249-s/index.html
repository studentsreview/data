Clio Area High School is located in Clio, MI. Students and parents can contact Clio Area High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (810) 591-1358. Clio Area High School has 1100 total students, 597 male, 501 female. Of these students from Clio Area High School, 346 are ninth grade students, 298 are tenth grade students, 232 are eleventh grade students, and 224 are twelfth grade students. Clio Area High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 25:1.
