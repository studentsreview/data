Buddy Taylor Middle School is located in Palm Coast, FL. Students and parents can contact Buddy Taylor Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (386) 446-6700. Buddy Taylor Middle School has 1163 total students, 559 male, 566 female. Buddy Taylor Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
