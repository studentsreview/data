High Tech High School's Profile

High Tech High School is located in San Diego, CA. Students and parents can contact High Tech High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (818) 609-2640. High Tech High School has 315 total students, 202 male, 113 female. Of these students from High Tech High School, 97 are ninth grade students, 80 are tenth grade students, 65 are eleventh grade students, and 73 are twelfth grade students. High Tech High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
