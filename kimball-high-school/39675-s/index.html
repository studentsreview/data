Kimball High School is located in Royal Oak, MI. Students and parents can contact Kimball High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (517) 548-6206. Kimball High School has 2600 total students, 1333 male, 1267 female. Of these students from Kimball High School, 658 are ninth grade students, 640 are tenth grade students, 630 are eleventh grade students, and 666 are twelfth grade students. Kimball High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
