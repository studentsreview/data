Hinsdale Central High School's Profile

Hinsdale Central High School is located in Hinsdale, IL. Students and parents can contact Hinsdale Central High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (630) 570-8000. Hinsdale Central High School has 2624 total students, 1295 male, 1301 female. Of these students from Hinsdale Central High School, 644 are ninth grade students, 697 are tenth grade students, 656 are eleventh grade students, and 627 are twelfth grade students. Hinsdale Central High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
