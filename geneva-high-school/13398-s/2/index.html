Geneva High School is located in Geneva, NY. Students and parents can contact Geneva High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (315) 781-0402. Geneva High School has 784 total students, 371 male, 413 female. Of these students from Geneva High School, 226 are ninth grade students, 177 are tenth grade students, 162 are eleventh grade students, and 179 are twelfth grade students. Geneva High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
