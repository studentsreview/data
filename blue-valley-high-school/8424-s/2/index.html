Blue Valley High School is located in Stilwell, KS. Students and parents can contact Blue Valley High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (913) 239-3700. Blue Valley High School has 1517 total students, 782 male, 735 female. Of these students from Blue Valley High School, 409 are ninth grade students, 388 are tenth grade students, 351 are eleventh grade students, and 369 are twelfth grade students. Blue Valley High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
