Claremont High School's Profile

Claremont High School is located in Claremont, CA. Students and parents can contact Claremont High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (909) 624-9053. Claremont High School has 2200 total students, 1084 male, 1116 female. Of these students from Claremont High School, 597 are ninth grade students, 537 are tenth grade students, 544 are eleventh grade students, and 522 are twelfth grade students. Claremont High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
