Burlington Community High Scho is located in Burlington, IA. Students and parents can contact Burlington Community High Scho by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (319) 753-2211. Burlington Community High Scho has 1308 total students, 677 male, 631 female. Of these students from Burlington Community High Scho, 351 are ninth grade students, 308 are tenth grade students, 300 are eleventh grade students, and 349 are twelfth grade students.
