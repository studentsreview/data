Brockton High School's Profile

Brockton High School is located in Brockton, MA. Students and parents can contact Brockton High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (508) 581-1640. Brockton High School has 400 total students, 174 male, 219 female. Of these students from Brockton High School, 97 are ninth grade students, 95 are tenth grade students, 104 are eleventh grade students, and 104 are twelfth grade students. Brockton High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
