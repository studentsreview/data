Florida employs over 247,327 teachers who oversee approximately 2,664,269 students. Students from Florida submitted more than 332,853 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Florida is 4.47, which happens to be above the national average of 4.33. Miami Jackson Senior High School, Desoto County High School, and Dixie M. Hollins High School are schools in Florida with positive teacher reviews.
