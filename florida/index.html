Florida employs over 248,846 teachers who oversee approximately 2,664,269 students. Students in Florida submitted more than 328,324 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Florida is 4.54, which happens to be above the national average of 4.46. Desoto County High School, Chamberlain High School, and East Bay High School are schools in Florida with positive teacher reviews.
