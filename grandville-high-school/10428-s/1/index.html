Grandville High School is located in Grandville, MI. Students and parents can contact Grandville High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (616) 254-6430. Grandville High School has 1880 total students, 963 male, 914 female. Of these students from Grandville High School, 468 are ninth grade students, 477 are tenth grade students, 443 are eleventh grade students, and 473 are twelfth grade students. Grandville High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
