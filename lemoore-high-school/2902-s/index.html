Lemoore High School's Profile

Lemoore High School is located in Lemoore, CA. Students and parents can contact Lemoore High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (559) 924-6600. Lemoore High School has 2020 total students, 996 male, 1024 female. Of these students from Lemoore High School, 523 are ninth grade students, 547 are tenth grade students, 480 are eleventh grade students, and 470 are twelfth grade students. Lemoore High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
