Brockport High School's Profile

Brockport High School is located in Brockport, NY. Students and parents can contact Brockport High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (585) 637-1877. Brockport High School has 1488 total students, 723 male, 755 female. Of these students from Brockport High School, 381 are ninth grade students, 363 are tenth grade students, 353 are eleventh grade students, and 387 are twelfth grade students. Brockport High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
