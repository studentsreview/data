Cornwall Central High School is located in Cornwall, NY. Students and parents can contact Cornwall Central High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (845) 534-8009. Cornwall Central High School has 1134 total students, 594 male, 540 female. Of these students from Cornwall Central High School, 274 are ninth grade students, 299 are tenth grade students, 285 are eleventh grade students, and 276 are twelfth grade students. Cornwall Central High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
