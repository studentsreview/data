Bridgeport High School's Profile

Bridgeport High School is located in Bridgeport, MI. Students and parents can contact Bridgeport High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (989) 777-3100. Bridgeport High School has 525 total students, 256 male, 269 female. Of these students from Bridgeport High School, 172 are ninth grade students, 128 are tenth grade students, 143 are eleventh grade students, and 82 are twelfth grade students. Bridgeport High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
