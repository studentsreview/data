Lisle Junior High School's Profile

Lisle Junior High School is located in Lisle, IL. Students and parents can contact Lisle Junior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (630) 493-8201. Lisle Junior High School has 361 total students, 192 male, 156 female. Lisle Junior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
