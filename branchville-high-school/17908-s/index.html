Branchville High School's Profile

Branchville High School is located in Branchville, SC. Students and parents can contact Branchville High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (803) 274-8875. Branchville High School has 293 total students, 147 male, 143 female. Of these students from Branchville High School, 53 are ninth grade students, 54 are tenth grade students, 43 are eleventh grade students, and 44 are twelfth grade students. Branchville High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
