Lasalle Springs Middle School's Profile

Lasalle Springs Middle School is located in St. Louis, MO. Students and parents can contact Lasalle Springs Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (636) 938-2425. Lasalle Springs Middle School has 870 total students, 450 male, 420 female. Lasalle Springs Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
