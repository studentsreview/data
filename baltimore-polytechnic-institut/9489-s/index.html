Baltimore Polytechnic Institut is located in Baltimore, MD. Students and parents can contact Baltimore Polytechnic Institut by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (410) 396-7026. Baltimore Polytechnic Institut has 1344 total students, 657 male, 687 female.
