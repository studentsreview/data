Largo High School's Profile

Largo High School is located in Upper Marlboro, MD. Students and parents can contact Largo High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (301) 808-8880. Largo High School has 1468 total students, 745 male, 723 female. Of these students from Largo High School, 401 are ninth grade students, 364 are tenth grade students, 334 are eleventh grade students, and 369 are twelfth grade students. Largo High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
