Escobedo Middle School is located in Las Vegas, NV. Students and parents can contact Escobedo Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (775) 738-7236. Escobedo Middle School has 692 total students, 384 male, 308 female. Escobedo Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
