Brighton Middle School is located in Rochester Ny, NY. Students and parents can contact Brighton Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (716) 792-2189. Brighton Middle School has 377 total students, 197 male, 180 female. Of these students from Brighton Middle School, 52 are ninth grade students, 60 are tenth grade students, 51 are eleventh grade students, and 57 are twelfth grade students.
