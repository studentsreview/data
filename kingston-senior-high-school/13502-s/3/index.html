Kingston Senior High School's Profile

Kingston Senior High School is located in Kingston, NY. Students and parents can contact Kingston Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (845) 331-1970. Kingston Senior High School has 2362 total students, 1200 male, 1162 female. Of these students from Kingston Senior High School, 687 are ninth grade students, 578 are tenth grade students, 575 are eleventh grade students, and 485 are twelfth grade students. Kingston Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
