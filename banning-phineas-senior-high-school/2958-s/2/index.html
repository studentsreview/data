Banning (phineas) Senior High School is located in Wilmington, CA. Students and parents can contact Banning (phineas) Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (310) 847-3700. Banning (phineas) Senior High School has 3495 total students, 1808 male, 1687 female. Of these students from Banning (phineas) Senior High School, 1342 are ninth grade students, 934 are tenth grade students, 725 are eleventh grade students, and 494 are twelfth grade students.
