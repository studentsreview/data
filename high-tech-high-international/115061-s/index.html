High Tech High International is located in San Diego, CA. Students and parents can contact High Tech High International by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (619) 398-4900. High Tech High International has 387 total students, 181 male, 206 female. Of these students from High Tech High International, 98 are ninth grade students, 98 are tenth grade students, 95 are eleventh grade students, and 96 are twelfth grade students.
