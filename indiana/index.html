Indiana employs over 87,568 teachers who oversee approximately 1,031,724 students. Students in Indiana submitted more than 78,265 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Indiana is 4.51, which happens to be above the national average of 4.46. Eastbrook High School, Merrillville High School, and Terre Haute South Vigo High School are schools in Indiana with positive teacher reviews.
