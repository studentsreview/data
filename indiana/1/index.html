Indiana employs over 79,947 teachers who oversee approximately 1,031,724 students. Students from Indiana submitted more than 79,507 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Indiana is 4.37, which happens to be above the national average of 4.32. Terre Haute South Vigo High School, Warren Central High School, and Whitko High School are schools in Indiana with positive teacher reviews.
