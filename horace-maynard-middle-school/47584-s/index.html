Horace Maynard Middle School's Profile

Horace Maynard Middle School is located in Maynardville, TN. Students and parents can contact Horace Maynard Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (865) 992-1030. Horace Maynard Middle School has 651 total students, 319 male, 332 female. Horace Maynard Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
