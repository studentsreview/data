Chelsea High School's Profile

Chelsea High School is located in Chelsea, MI. Students and parents can contact Chelsea High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (734) 433-2201. Chelsea High School has 957 total students, 493 male, 464 female. Of these students from Chelsea High School, 229 are ninth grade students, 243 are tenth grade students, 244 are eleventh grade students, and 241 are twelfth grade students. Chelsea High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
