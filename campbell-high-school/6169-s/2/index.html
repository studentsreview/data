Campbell High School's Profile

Campbell High School is located in Ewa Beach, HI. Students and parents can contact Campbell High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (808) 689-1200. Campbell High School has 2491 total students, 1293 male, 1198 female. Of these students from Campbell High School, 653 are ninth grade students, 675 are tenth grade students, 628 are eleventh grade students, and 525 are twelfth grade students. Campbell High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
