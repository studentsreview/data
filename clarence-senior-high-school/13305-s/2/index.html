Clarence Senior High School's Profile

Clarence Senior High School is located in Clarence, NY. Students and parents can contact Clarence Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (716) 407-9020. Clarence Senior High School has 1688 total students, 885 male, 803 female. Of these students from Clarence Senior High School, 407 are ninth grade students, 439 are tenth grade students, 395 are eleventh grade students, and 447 are twelfth grade students. Clarence Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
