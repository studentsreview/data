Lee's Summit West High School is located in Lee's Summit, MO. Students and parents can contact Lee's Summit West High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (816) 986-4000. Lee's Summit West High School has 1556 total students, 777 male, 779 female. Of these students from Lee's Summit West High School, 452 are ninth grade students, 386 are tenth grade students, 363 are eleventh grade students, and 355 are twelfth grade students. Lee's Summit West High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
