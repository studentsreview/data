Hudson High School is located in Hudson, OH. Students and parents can contact Hudson High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (330) 653-1416. Hudson High School has 1754 total students, 920 male, 812 female. Of these students from Hudson High School, 427 are ninth grade students, 454 are tenth grade students, 450 are eleventh grade students, and 423 are twelfth grade students. Hudson High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
