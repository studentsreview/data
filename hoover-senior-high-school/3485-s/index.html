Hoover Senior High School's Profile

Hoover Senior High School is located in San Diego, CA. Students and parents can contact Hoover Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (818) 242-6801. Hoover Senior High School has 2260 total students, 1122 male, 1138 female. Of these students from Hoover Senior High School, 519 are ninth grade students, 599 are tenth grade students, 539 are eleventh grade students, and 603 are twelfth grade students. Hoover Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
