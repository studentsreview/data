Chesterton Senior High School's Profile

Chesterton Senior High School is located in Chesterton, IN. Students and parents can contact Chesterton Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (219) 983-3730. Chesterton Senior High School has 2025 total students, 1012 male, 973 female. Of these students from Chesterton Senior High School, 519 are ninth grade students, 497 are tenth grade students, 536 are eleventh grade students, and 473 are twelfth grade students. Chesterton Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
