Lawrence High School is located in Lawrence, KS. Students and parents can contact Lawrence High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (785) 354-9798. Lawrence High School has 213 total students, 184 male, 28 female. Of these students from Lawrence High School, 64 are ninth grade students, 67 are tenth grade students, 45 are eleventh grade students, and 33 are twelfth grade students. Lawrence High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 7:1.
