Christiansburg High School's Profile

Christiansburg High School is located in Christiansburg, VA. Students and parents can contact Christiansburg High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (540) 382-5178. Christiansburg High School has 1036 total students, 526 male, 499 female. Of these students from Christiansburg High School, 298 are ninth grade students, 262 are tenth grade students, 248 are eleventh grade students, and 228 are twelfth grade students. Christiansburg High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
