Iowa employs over 7,037 teachers who oversee approximately 474,235 students. Students in Iowa submitted more than 18,645 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Iowa is 4.35, which happens to be below the national average of 4.46. Bettendorf High School, Prairie High School, and Abraham Lincoln High School are schools in Iowa with positive teacher reviews.
