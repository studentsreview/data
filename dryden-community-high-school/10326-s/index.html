Dryden Community High School

Total Teachers: 29

Dryden Community High School is located in Dryden, MI. Students and parents can contact Dryden Community High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (734) 994-2021. Dryden Community High School has 466 total students, 205 male, 236 female. Of these students from Dryden Community High School, 109 are ninth grade students, 120 are tenth grade students, 116 are eleventh grade students, and 119 are twelfth grade students. Dryden Community High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
