Highland High School's Profile

Highland High School is located in Highland, IN. Students and parents can contact Highland High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (219) 922-5610. Highland High School has 1209 total students, 598 male, 599 female. Of these students from Highland High School, 350 are ninth grade students, 323 are tenth grade students, 289 are eleventh grade students, and 247 are twelfth grade students. Highland High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
