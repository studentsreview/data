Brownsville Area High School is located in Brownsville, PA. Students and parents can contact Brownsville Area High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (724) 785-8200. Brownsville Area High School has 600 total students, 290 male, 308 female. Of these students from Brownsville Area High School, 184 are ninth grade students, 140 are tenth grade students, 148 are eleventh grade students, and 128 are twelfth grade students. Brownsville Area High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
