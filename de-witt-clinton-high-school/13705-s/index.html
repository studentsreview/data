De Witt Clinton High School is located in Bronx, NY. Students and parents can contact De Witt Clinton High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 543-1000. De Witt Clinton High School has 4398 total students, 2076 male, 2322 female. Of these students from De Witt Clinton High School, 1352 are ninth grade students, 1077 are tenth grade students, 863 are eleventh grade students, and 688 are twelfth grade students.
