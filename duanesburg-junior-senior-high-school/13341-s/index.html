Duanesburg Junior Senior High School

Delanson, New York

Facebook Share
  • Total Teachers: 69

Duanesburg Junior Senior High School is located in Delanson, NY. Students and parents can contact Duanesburg Junior Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (845) 434-6800. Duanesburg Junior Senior High School has 685 total students, 333 male, 352 female. Of these students from Duanesburg Junior Senior High School, 130 are ninth grade students, 93 are tenth grade students, 126 are eleventh grade students, and 113 are twelfth grade students. Duanesburg Junior Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 11:1.
