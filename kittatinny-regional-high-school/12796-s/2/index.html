Kittatinny Regional High School

Total Teachers: 175
Avg. Teacher Rating: 3.8
School Ratings: 1773
Kittatinny Regional High School is located in Newton, NJ. Students and parents can contact Kittatinny Regional High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (973) 383-1800. Kittatinny Regional High School has 1186 total students, 607 male, 579 female. Of these students from Kittatinny Regional High School, 175 are ninth grade students, 216 are tenth grade students, 184 are eleventh grade students, and 216 are twelfth grade students. Kittatinny Regional High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
