Fraser High School is located in Fraser, MI. Students and parents can contact Fraser High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (586) 439-7200. Fraser High School has 1668 total students, 804 male, 844 female. Of these students from Fraser High School, 386 are ninth grade students, 445 are tenth grade students, 413 are eleventh grade students, and 424 are twelfth grade students. Fraser High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
