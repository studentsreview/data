Del Webb Middle School is located in Henderson, NV. Students and parents can contact Del Webb Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (702) 799-3120. Del Webb Middle School has 1514 total students, 801 male, 713 female. Del Webb Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 26:1.
