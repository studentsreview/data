Lamar High School's Profile

Lamar High School is located in Houston, TX. Students and parents can contact Lamar High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (281) 985-6330. Lamar High School has 2305 total students, 1141 male, 1164 female. Of these students from Lamar High School, 191 are ninth grade students, 968 are tenth grade students, 525 are eleventh grade students, and 621 are twelfth grade students. Lamar High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
