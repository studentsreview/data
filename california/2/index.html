California employs over 167,299 teachers who oversee approximately 6,209,604 students. Students from California submitted more than 1,002,229 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in California is 4.24, which happens to be below the national average of 4.34. Lower Lake High School, Alain Leroy Locke College Prep Academy, and Bidwell Junior High School are schools in California with positive teacher reviews.
