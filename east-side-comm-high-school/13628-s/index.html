East Side Comm High School's Profile

East Side Comm High School is located in New York, NY. Students and parents can contact East Side Comm High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (212) 460-8467. East Side Comm High School has 558 total students, 290 male, 268 female. Of these students from East Side Comm High School, 83 are ninth grade students, 75 are tenth grade students, 62 are eleventh grade students, and 67 are twelfth grade students. East Side Comm High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
