Arizona employs over 19,580 teachers who oversee approximately 1,087,340 students. Students in Arizona submitted more than 63,810 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Arizona is 4.37, which happens to be below the national average of 4.46. Dobson High School, Skyline High School, and Central High School are schools in Arizona with positive teacher reviews.
