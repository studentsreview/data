Garfield (james A.) Senior High School's Profile

Garfield (james A.) Senior High School is located in Los Angeles, CA. Students and parents can contact Garfield (james A.) Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (323) 268-9361. Garfield (james A.) Senior High School has 4595 total students, 2372 male, 2223 female. Of these students from Garfield (james A.) Senior High School, 1751 are ninth grade students, 1207 are tenth grade students, 989 are eleventh grade students, and 648 are twelfth grade students. Garfield (james A.) Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
