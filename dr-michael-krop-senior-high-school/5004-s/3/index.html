Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School's Profile

Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School is located in Miami, FL. Students and parents can contact Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (305) 652-6808. Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School has 3771 total students, 1845 male, 1854 female. Of these students from Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School, 1133 are ninth grade students, 1071 are tenth grade students, 804 are eleventh grade students, and 763 are twelfth grade students. Dr. Michael Krop Senior High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 22:1.
