Dulaney High School's Profile

Dulaney High School is located in Timonium, MD. Students and parents can contact Dulaney High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (410) 887-7633. Dulaney High School has 1924 total students, 946 male, 978 female. Of these students from Dulaney High School, 468 are ninth grade students, 470 are tenth grade students, 480 are eleventh grade students, and 506 are twelfth grade students. Dulaney High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
