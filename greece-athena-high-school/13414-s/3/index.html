Greece-athena High School's Profile

Greece-athena High School is located in Rochester, NY. Students and parents can contact Greece-athena High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (585) 966-4000. Greece-athena High School has 1368 total students, 665 male, 703 female. Of these students from Greece-athena High School, 325 are ninth grade students, 310 are tenth grade students, 319 are eleventh grade students, and 392 are twelfth grade students. Greece-athena High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
