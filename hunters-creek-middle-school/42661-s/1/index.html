Hunters Creek Middle School's Profile

Hunters Creek Middle School is located in Orlando, FL. Students and parents can contact Hunters Creek Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (407) 858-4620. Hunters Creek Middle School has 1066 total students, 494 male, 525 female. Hunters Creek Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
