Lakeview High School's Profile

Lakeview High School is located in Cortland, OH. Students and parents can contact Lakeview High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (330) 637-4921. Lakeview High School has 690 total students, 355 male, 328 female. Of these students from Lakeview High School, 161 are ninth grade students, 163 are tenth grade students, 173 are eleventh grade students, and 193 are twelfth grade students. Lakeview High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
