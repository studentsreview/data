Lakeview Middle School's Profile

Lakeview Middle School is located in Wintergarden, FL. Students and parents can contact Lakeview Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (407) 877-5010. Lakeview Middle School has 1285 total students, 627 male, 624 female. Lakeview Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
