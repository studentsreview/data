Heard County High School's Profile

Heard County High School is located in Franklin, GA. Students and parents can contact Heard County High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (706) 675-3320. Heard County High School has 647 total students, 325 male, 310 female. Of these students from Heard County High School, 210 are ninth grade students, 158 are tenth grade students, 145 are eleventh grade students, and 134 are twelfth grade students. Heard County High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
