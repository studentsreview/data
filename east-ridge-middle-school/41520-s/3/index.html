East Ridge Middle School's Profile

East Ridge Middle School is located in Ridgefield, CT. Students and parents can contact East Ridge Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (203) 438-3744. East Ridge Middle School has 743 total students, 401 male, 342 female. East Ridge Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
