Leadership High School's Profile

Leadership High School is located in San Jose, CA. Students and parents can contact Leadership High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (415) 841-8910. Leadership High School has 239 total students, 120 male, 119 female. Of these students from Leadership High School, 37 are ninth grade students, 52 are tenth grade students, 77 are eleventh grade students, and 73 are twelfth grade students. Leadership High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 11:1.
