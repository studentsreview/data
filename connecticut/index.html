Connecticut employs over 41,528 teachers who oversee approximately 529,098 students. Students in Connecticut submitted more than 230,223 teacher and professor ratings through RateMyTeachers.com. The average teacher rating in Connecticut is 4.35, which happens to be below the national average of 4.46. Central High School, West Haven High School, and Windham High School are schools in Connecticut with positive teacher reviews.
