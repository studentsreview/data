Grapevine High School is located in Grapevine, TX. Students and parents can contact Grapevine High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (830) 851-0895. Grapevine High School has 551 total students, 281 male, 270 female. Of these students from Grapevine High School, 144 are ninth grade students, 134 are tenth grade students, 133 are eleventh grade students, and 140 are twelfth grade students. Grapevine High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
