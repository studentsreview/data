Griffith Inst High School's Profile

Griffith Inst High School is located in Springville, NY. Students and parents can contact Griffith Inst High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (716) 592-3202. Griffith Inst High School has 734 total students, 372 male, 362 female. Of these students from Griffith Inst High School, 188 are ninth grade students, 170 are tenth grade students, 177 are eleventh grade students, and 199 are twelfth grade students. Griffith Inst High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
