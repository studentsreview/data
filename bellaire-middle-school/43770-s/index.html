Bellaire Middle School is located in Bellaire, OH. Students and parents can contact Bellaire Middle School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (740) 676-1635. Bellaire Middle School has 432 total students, 211 male, 209 female. Bellaire Middle School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 12:1.
