Liberty High School is located in Liberty, NY. Students and parents can contact Liberty High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (845) 292-5400. Liberty High School has 527 total students, 275 male, 252 female. Of these students from Liberty High School, 137 are ninth grade students, 150 are tenth grade students, 119 are eleventh grade students, and 121 are twelfth grade students. Liberty High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 10:1.
