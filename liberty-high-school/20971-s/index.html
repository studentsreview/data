Bealeton, Virginia

Facebook Share

Liberty High School is located in Bealeton, VA. Students and parents can contact Liberty High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (540) 586-2541. Liberty High School has 1043 total students, 541 male, 502 female. Of these students from Liberty High School, 275 are ninth grade students, 244 are tenth grade students, 270 are eleventh grade students, and 254 are twelfth grade students. Liberty High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
