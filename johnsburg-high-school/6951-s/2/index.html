Johnsburg High School is located in Johnsburg, IL. Students and parents can contact Johnsburg High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (815) 385-9233. Johnsburg High School has 907 total students, 451 male, 453 female. Johnsburg High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
