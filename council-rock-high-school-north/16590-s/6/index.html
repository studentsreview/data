Council Rock High School North's Profile

Council Rock High School North is located in Newtown, PA. Students and parents can contact Council Rock High School North by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (215) 944-1300. Council Rock High School North has 2278 total students, 1183 male, 1094 female. Of these students from Council Rock High School North, 550 are ninth grade students, 567 are tenth grade students, 558 are eleventh grade students, and 597 are twelfth grade students. Council Rock High School North maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
