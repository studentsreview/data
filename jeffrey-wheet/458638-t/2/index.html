    I took Mr. Wheet's Art Major class for the first four years that he taught at Unionville, 1984-1988. While his approach at that time didn't really prepare students for a real career in the visual arts, I found him to be often very inspiring on a personal level. Several things that he said have stuck with me as valuable life lessons. If you have any interest in opening your mind and seeing that there is a big world outside of the soul-crushing uniformity that is Unionville, take his class.
