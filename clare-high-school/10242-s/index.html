Clare High School's Profile

Clare High School is located in Clare, MI. Students and parents can contact Clare High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (989) 386-7789. Clare High School has 473 total students, 238 male, 234 female. Of these students from Clare High School, 132 are ninth grade students, 113 are tenth grade students, 120 are eleventh grade students, and 108 are twelfth grade students. Clare High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
