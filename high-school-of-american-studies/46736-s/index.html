High School Of American Studies's Profile

High School Of American Studies is located in Bronx, NY. Students and parents can contact High School Of American Studies by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (718) 329-2144. High School Of American Studies has 332 total students, 170 male, 162 female. Of these students from High School Of American Studies, 89 are ninth grade students, 82 are tenth grade students, 75 are eleventh grade students, and 86 are twelfth grade students. High School Of American Studies maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
