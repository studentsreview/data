Colts Neck High School is located in Colts Neck, NJ. Students and parents can contact Colts Neck High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (732) 761-0190. Colts Neck High School has 1709 total students, 839 male, 870 female. Of these students from Colts Neck High School, 385 are ninth grade students, 361 are tenth grade students, 462 are eleventh grade students, and 482 are twelfth grade students. Colts Neck High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
