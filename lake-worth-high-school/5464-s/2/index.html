Lake Worth High School is located in Lake Worth, FL. Students and parents can contact Lake Worth High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (561) 533-6300. Lake Worth High School has 2200 total students, 955 male, 1172 female. Of these students from Lake Worth High School, 633 are ninth grade students, 540 are tenth grade students, 542 are eleventh grade students, and 485 are twelfth grade students. Lake Worth High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
