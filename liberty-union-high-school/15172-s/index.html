Liberty Union High School's Profile

Liberty Union High School is located in Baltimore, OH. Students and parents can contact Liberty Union High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (740) 862-4107. Liberty Union High School has 439 total students, 215 male, 223 female. Of these students from Liberty Union High School, 100 are ninth grade students, 128 are tenth grade students, 104 are eleventh grade students, and 107 are twelfth grade students. Liberty Union High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
