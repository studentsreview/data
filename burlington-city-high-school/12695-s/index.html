Burlington City High School's Profile

Burlington City High School is located in Burlington, NJ. Students and parents can contact Burlington City High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (609) 387-5800. Burlington City High School has 812 total students, 419 male, 393 female. Of these students from Burlington City High School, 180 are ninth grade students, 138 are tenth grade students, 136 are eleventh grade students, and 154 are twelfth grade students. Burlington City High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 11:1.
