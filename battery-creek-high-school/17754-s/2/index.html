Battery Creek High School's Profile

Battery Creek High School is located in Beaufort, SC. Students and parents can contact Battery Creek High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (843) 322-5500. Battery Creek High School has 1386 total students, 693 male, 692 female. Of these students from Battery Creek High School, 529 are ninth grade students, 356 are tenth grade students, 241 are eleventh grade students, and 260 are twelfth grade students. Battery Creek High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
