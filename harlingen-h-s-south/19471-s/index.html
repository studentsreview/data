Harlingen H S - South's Profile

Harlingen H S - South is located in Harlingen, TX. Students and parents can contact Harlingen H S - South by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (956) 427-3800. Harlingen H S - South has 2100 total students, 1053 male, 1047 female. Of these students from Harlingen H S - South, 710 are ninth grade students, 478 are tenth grade students, 493 are eleventh grade students, and 419 are twelfth grade students.
