Coldwater High School's Profile

Coldwater High School is located in Coldwater, MI. Students and parents can contact Coldwater High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (517) 279-5930. Coldwater High School has 962 total students, 507 male, 455 female. Of these students from Coldwater High School, 244 are ninth grade students, 241 are tenth grade students, 234 are eleventh grade students, and 243 are twelfth grade students. Coldwater High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
