Bassett High School's Profile

Bassett High School is located in Bassett, VA. Students and parents can contact Bassett High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (276) 629-1731. Bassett High School has 1240 total students, 620 male, 620 female. Of these students from Bassett High School, 384 are ninth grade students, 300 are tenth grade students, 286 are eleventh grade students, and 270 are twelfth grade students. Bassett High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
