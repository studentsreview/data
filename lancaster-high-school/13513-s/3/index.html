Lancaster High School is located in Lancaster, NY. Students and parents can contact Lancaster High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (716) 686-3250. Lancaster High School has 2055 total students, 1041 male, 1014 female. Of these students from Lancaster High School, 513 are ninth grade students, 518 are tenth grade students, 519 are eleventh grade students, and 505 are twelfth grade students.
