Lancaster High School is located in Lancaster, OH. Students and parents can contact Lancaster High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (740) 681-7500. Lancaster High School has 1778 total students, 931 male, 824 female. Of these students from Lancaster High School, 455 are ninth grade students, 463 are tenth grade students, 444 are eleventh grade students, and 416 are twelfth grade students. Lancaster High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
