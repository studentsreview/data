Lancaster High School

Total Teachers: 142
Avg. Teacher Rating: 4.3
School Ratings: 393
Lancaster High School is located in Lancaster, CA. Students and parents can contact Lancaster High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (661) 726-7649. Lancaster High School has 3200 total students, 1547 male, 1653 female. Of these students from Lancaster High School, 838 are ninth grade students, 887 are tenth grade students, 701 are eleventh grade students, and 774 are twelfth grade students. Lancaster High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
