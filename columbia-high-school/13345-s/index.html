Columbia High School's Profile

Columbia High School is located in East Greenbush, NY. Students and parents can contact Columbia High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (518) 207-2000. Columbia High School has 1541 total students, 741 male, 798 female. Of these students from Columbia High School, 378 are ninth grade students, 426 are tenth grade students, 372 are eleventh grade students, and 365 are twelfth grade students. Columbia High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 14:1.
