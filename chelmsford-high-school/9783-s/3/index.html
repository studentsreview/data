Chelmsford High School's Profile

Chelmsford High School is located in North Chelmsford, MA. Students and parents can contact Chelmsford High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (978) 251-5111. Chelmsford High School has 1678 total students, 823 male, 853 female. Of these students from Chelmsford High School, 415 are ninth grade students, 402 are tenth grade students, 430 are eleventh grade students, and 429 are twelfth grade students. Chelmsford High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
